*Lucas Yunkyu Lee,Soonho Kim,Youngwook Kim,Sangmin Kim,Jaesik Park*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: 本文通过系统分析4D高斯溅射（4DGS）框架中的隐藏因素，建立了可控基线FreeTimeGS_ours，揭示了时间分区和光度-时空一致性差异等关键机制，并提出了改进方法FreeTimeGS++（采用门控边缘化和神经速度场），实现了更稳定和鲁棒的动态场景重建。

Method: 1）建立可控基线FreeTimeGS_ours，形式化并复现FreeTimeGS的启发式方法；2）沿基本轴解构4DGS，分析高斯持续时间引发的时间分区、光度保真度与时空一致性之间的差异；3）提出FreeTimeGS++，采用门控边缘化和神经速度场。

Abstract: The recent surge in 4D Gaussian Splatting (4DGS) has achieved impressive dynamic scene reconstruction. While these methods demonstrate remarkable performance, the specific drivers behind such gains remain less explored, making a systematic understanding of the underlying principles challenging. We first establish a controlled baseline, FreeTimeGS_ours, by formalizing and reproducing the heuristics of the state-of-the-art FreeTimeGS. ### [2] [Mix3R: Mixing Feed-forward Reconstruction and Generative 3D Priors for Joint Multi-view Aligned 3D Reconstruction and Pose Estimation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03359)
*Siyou Lin,Zhou Xue,Hongwen Zhang,Liang An,Dongping Li,Shaohui Jiao,Yebin Liu*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: Mix3R是一种将前馈重建与3D生成融合到统一框架中的方法，通过两阶段生成（稀疏体素生成+纹理几何生成）和混合Transformer架构，在保持输入对齐的同时生成完整3D形状，并在相机位姿估计上优于纯前馈方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有稀疏视角3D重建存在两条路线：前馈重建预测像素对齐的点图但缺少完整几何，生成式3D重建生成完整几何但输入对齐差。Mix3R旨在将两者优势结合，实现既完整又对齐的3D重建。

Method: 两阶段生成：1）稀疏体素生成阶段，联合生成粗3D结构、每视角点图和相机参数，采用Mixture-of-Transformers架构融合前馈重建模型和3D生成模型的预训练先验；2）纹理几何生成阶段，利用重叠注意力偏置以无训练方式将输入纹理正确放置到生成形状上。

Result: 与纯3D生成方法相比，生成的3D形状具有更好的输入对齐；与前馈重建方法相比，相机位姿估计更准确。两个分支相互受益：前馈分支学习基于生成式3D先验进行预测，生成分支则从前馈分支获取几何信息特征。

Conclusion: Mix3R成功在前馈重建与3D生成之间建立了互惠关系，证明了两种范式可以在统一框架中对齐协同工作，兼顾几何完整性与输入对齐性。

Abstract: Recent trends in sparse-view 3D reconstruction have taken two different paths: feed-forward reconstruction that predicts pixel-aligned point maps without a complete geometry, and generative 3D reconstruction that generates complete geometry but often with poor input-alignment. We present Mix3R, a novel generative 3D reconstruction method which mixes feed-forward reconstruction and 3D generation into a single framework in an aligned manner. Mix3R generates a 3D shape in two stages: a sparse voxel generation stage and a textured geometry generation stage. Unlike pure generative methods, our first-stage generation jointly produces a coarse 3D structure (sparse voxels), per-view point maps and camera parameters aligned to that 3D structure. This is made possible by introducing a Mixture-of-Transformers architecture that inserts global self-attentions to a feed-forward reconstruction model and a 3D generative model, both pretrained on large-scale data. This design effectively retains the pretrained priors but enables better 2D-3D alignment. Based on the initial aligned generations of sparse 3D voxels and point maps, we compute an overlap-based attention bias that is directly added to another pretrained textured geometry generation model, enabling it to correctly place input textures onto generated shapes in a training-free manner. Our design brings mutual benefits to both feed-forward reconstruction and 3D generation: The feed-forward branch learns to ground its predictions to a generative 3D prior, and conversely, the 3D generation branch is conditioned on geometrically informative features from the feed-forward branch. As a result, our method produces 3D shapes with better input alignment compared with pure 3D generative methods, together with camera pose estimations more accurate than previous feed-forward reconstruction methods. Our project page is at https://jsnln.github.io/mix3r/

### [3] [First Shape, Then Meaning: Efficient Geometry and Semantics Learning for Indoor Reconstruction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03463)

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: 本文提出FSTM，一种通过几何预热+语义场估计的两步法实现神经表面重建与语义分割的统一框架，比多SDF方法训练快2.3倍且效果更优。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有神经表面重建方法虽能集成语义标签，但通常继承多SDF学习的训练速度慢、可扩展性差的问题，需要更高效统一的几何与语义联合学习方案。

Method: 采用两阶段流程：第一阶段使用RGB输入和几何线索进行几何预热（geometry warm-up），优化几何表示；第二阶段进行语义场估计（semantic field estimation），不依赖专用模块或复杂多SDF设计。

Result: 在Replica数据集上训练速度提升2.3倍，在ScanNet++上对真实世界不完美性具有更高鲁棒性，且通过恢复更多物体表面实现更高召回率，优于多SDF方法。

Abstract: Neural Surface Reconstruction has become a standard methodology for indoor 3D reconstruction, with Signed Distance Functions (SDFs) proving particularly effective for representing scene geometry. A variety of applications require a detailed understanding of the scene context, driving the need for object-level semantic signals. While recent methods successfully integrate semantic labels, they often inherit the slow training time and limited scalability of multi-SDF learning. In this paper, we introduce FSTM, a unified approach for learning geometry and semantics through a two-step process: a geometry warm-up using RGB inputs and geometric cues, followed by semantic field estimation. By first optimising geometry without semantic supervision, we observe substantial improvements compared to the standard joint optimisation. Rather than relying on specialised modules or complex multi-SDF designs, FSTM shows that a streamlined formulation is sufficient to achieve strong geometric and semantic reconstructions. Experiments on both synthetic and real-world indoor datasets show that our method outperforms multi-SDF approaches. It trains 2.3x faster on Replica, improves robustness to real-world imperfections on ScanNet++, and achieves higher recall by recovering the surfaces of more objects in the scene. The code will be made available at https://remichierchia.github.io/FSTM.

### [4] [AniMatrix: An Anime Video Generation Model that Thinks in Art, Not Physics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03652)
*Tencent HY Team*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: AniMatrix是一个面向动漫视频生成的专业模型，通过双通道条件机制和三步训练策略（重新定义正确性、覆盖物理先验、区分艺术与故障），解决物理偏置模型在动漫艺术表达上的不足，在专业动画师评估的五个维度中四项排名第一。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有视频生成模型天然偏向物理真实感，但动漫故意违反物理规律（如拉伸、冲击帧、Q版变形），且数千种共存的艺术风格不存在统一的"动漫物理"可供模型学习，导致物理偏置模型要么抹平艺术性，要么在风格多样性下崩溃。

Method: 1) 双通道条件机制：Production Knowledge System编码可控制作变量（风格、运动、镜头、特效），AniCaption从像素推断导演指令；可训练标签编码器保留分类结构，冻结T5编码器处理自由文本，通过交叉注意力（细粒度控制）和AdaLN调制（全局强制）双路径注入。2) 风格-运动-变形课程学习逐步从近物理运动过渡到完全动漫表现。3) 变形感知偏好优化结合领域奖励模型，区分有意艺术手法与病态崩溃。

Conclusion: AniMatrix通过结构性知识建模和渐进式训练，成功实现了面向艺术正确性而非物理正确性的动漫视频生成，并计划开源模型权重和推理代码。

Abstract: Video generation models internalize physical realism as their prior. Anime deliberately violates physics: smears, impact frames, chibi shifts; and its thousands of coexisting artistic conventions yield no single "physics of anime" a model can absorb. Physics-biased models therefore flatten the artistry that defines the medium or collapse under its stylistic variance. We present AniMatrix, a video generation model that targets artistic rather than physical correctness through a dual-channel conditioning mechanism and a three-step transition: redefine correctness, override the physics prior, and distinguish art from failure. First, a Production Knowledge System encodes anime as a structured taxonomy of controllable production variables (Style, Motion, Camera, VFX), and AniCaption infers these variables from pixels as directorial directives. A trainable tag encoder preserves the field-value structure of this taxonomy while a frozen T5 encoder handles free-form narrative; dual-path injection (cross-attention for fine-grained control, AdaLN modulation for global enforcement) ensures categorical directives are never diluted by open-ended text. Second, a style-motion-deformation curriculum transitions the model from near-physical motion to full anime expressiveness. Third, deformation-aware preference optimization with a domain-specific reward model separates intentional artistry from pathological collapse. On an anime-specific human evaluation with five production dimensions scored by professional animators, AniMatrix ranks first on four of five, with the largest gains over Seedance-Pro 1.0 on Prompt Understanding (+0.70, +22.4 percent) and Artistic Motion (+0.55, +16.9 percent). We will publicly release the AniMatrix model weights and inference code.
